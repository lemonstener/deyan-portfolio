About Me

I am a software engineer specializing in JavaScript and React

Hello and thank you for visiting my page! My name is Deyan, and I’m a Software Engineer based in the Greater St. Louis area.

With over 4 years of hands-on experience, I specialize in UI development using JavaScript and React. Additionally, I’ve successfully leveraged technologies like Node.js, C#, and Python for both Back-end and Full-stack development, applying them in both professional settings and personal projects.

As a continuous learner, I thrive on upskilling to meet new challenges, delivering immediate value to my team and organization. My adaptability has enabled me to save employers thousands in development costs over the years by quickly acquiring and applying new skills.

Currently, I’m seeking my next opportunity in either Front-end or Full-stack development. I’m drawn to innovative companies that tackle complex problems and prioritize the professional growth of their employees.

If you believe we could be a good fit, I’d love to connect!
