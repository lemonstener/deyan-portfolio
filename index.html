About Me

I am a T-shaped, Front-end Software Engineer based in the Greater Saint Louis area

Hello World! My name is Deyan and I am a Front-end Software Engineer. I am originally from Bulgaria and have lived in the United States since 2012.

I specialize in developing SPA applications with React and JavaScript, but my skillset extends well beyond that. I am also more than capable of producing quality back-end work when needed, as demonstrated by my work experience and personal projects.

I have always maintained a "Never get comfortable, always challenge yourself" mentality, which has helped me grow immensely in all of my roles. I enjoy tackling complex problems, crafting innovative solutions, and cracking a silly joke in-between.

If you like what you see, feel free to check out my resume or reach out to me directly! I am looking forward to hearing from you!
